AI Capabilities

AI integration is not a feature we offer — it is the foundation of how we build. Every system we architect is designed with the assumption that intelligence should be embedded, not added later.

Capability
What It Does
Business Impact
RAG System
(Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
Dynamic knowledge base trained on your product catalog, FAQs, and policies. Customer queries get accurate, contextual answers drawn directly from your data.
Eliminates repetitive CS queries. Scales product knowledge without manual updates.
AI Customer Service Chatbot
Custom-trained on your specific products, brand voice, and FAQ library. Handles common inquiries 24/7, escalates edge cases to your team.
Reduces CS workload by 60–80% on routine inquiries. Available across web, WhatsApp, and messaging channels.
Automation Workflows (n8n)
Connect your store to the tools you already use. Order notifications, CRM sync, inventory alerts, abandoned cart recovery, post-purchase email sequences.
Replaces manual operations. Scales without adding headcount.
Fine-Tuning (Enterprise)
Custom model training on your proprietary dataset. Available as a separate engagement after data audit and feasibility assessment.
Domain-specific accuracy beyond out-of-the-box models. For enterprises with significant training data.


A note on fine-tuning:
Fine-tuning is not suitable for every project. It requires substantial training data, compute resources, and clear use-case definition. We always conduct a data audit before proposing it — and we will tell you honestly if simpler approaches (RAG, prompt engineering) will serve you just as well.
